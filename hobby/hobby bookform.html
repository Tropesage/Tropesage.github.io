Civilization VI

Civilization 6 is the latestest entry in the Classic Civlization franchise. This entry seeks to shake up the formula by moving wonders and groups of buildings outside of the main city. In the above image you can see that the sciece district and it's buildings like libraries lie to the southwest of the city. In previous games they would be built in the city itself and would not be visible on the map.

In the above image you can also see that rhodes stands about two tiles away from the city.
